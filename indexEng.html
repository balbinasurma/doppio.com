QUEEN OF ITALIAN CUISINE OLIVE OIL FRANTOI CUTRERA Made in Italy, Sicily. The company is also trusted in Poland, which it has gained thanks to its quality of products. The Cutrera family has been cultivating olive trees themselves for many years, thanks to this, they obtain such excellent quality of the olive oils offered. The Cutrera Family's passion, nurtured over decades, has come to fruition amazing taste and quality of olive oil. All production stages performed are handmade with due care so that the fruit is not damaged during harvest. The origins of the company date back to the beginning of the 20th century, in 1906, near a small town in southeastern Sicily the first cultivation of this unusual variety of olive oil was established. Currently, the entire plantation of olive groves where olive oil is produced covers over 50 hectares.
