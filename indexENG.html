DOPPIO CAFFE SERVICE is a Polish company specializing in the HoReCa industry and servicing coffee machines of such brands as Lavazza, Saeco, De'Longhi, Siemens, Miele, Krups, Bosch. We are also an importer of Italian coffees and Cutrera olive oil. We have been a distributor of Italian Lavazza coffee and Miscela d'Oro coffee for over 10 years. We offer coffee beans, coffee in Nespresso capsules, Lavazza capsules and coffee in e.s.e. pods. Wholesale and retail sale of coffee. Our offer includes coffee machines for offices and catering. We currently employ highly qualified employees in the following departments: sales, customer service, service and accounting. Our young, professional team, regularly trained at manufacturers' offices, is guided by the principle of meeting customer needs. Thanks to many years of experience, we provide services of the highest quality. Many customers have already trusted us, you can also see that it is worth using our services! OUR GOAL is comprehensive customer service as part of the services provided.
